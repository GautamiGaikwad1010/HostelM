Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Providing eco-friendly, comfortable student hostel accommodation with a serene ambiance, is our business. With modern architectural designs and sustainable building materials used in our hostel facilities, it is our vision to be the number one preferred student hostel developers in Ghana. We aim to establish over 3000 bed hostels, with state-of-the-art amenities, in various tertiary institutions by 2025.

Our Mission

We intend to offer accommodation solution to bridge the gap created due to the high student population occasioned partly by the free senior high school program. Our mission is to provide hostel accommodation to solve the perennial challenges in various Tertiary institutions across the country.

What We Do

The increasing number of senior high school graduates who will seek admission into the various universities will require large investments in infrastructure, including hostels facilities to accommodation them. To help sovle this growing challenge, LEDing Hostels has been established as an affiliate of leding Ghana Limited to provide hostel facilities.

LEDing Hostels facilities are equipped with all the modern social amenities comparable to those found in commercial gated estates. Our hostels are designed primarily with the students comfort and well-being in mind.
